Does Population Have an Impact on Housing Prices?

Sparked by curiosity about the housing boom that seemed to occur during the covid-19 pandemic, we took a look at the data between January 2019 and December 2021 to find the relationship between housing prices and population. We will input this data into a Linear Regression Machine Learning Model to predict the housing prices for New York in future years.

Interactive Tableau Dashboard

As you can see in the charts below, the majority of houses on the Eastern edge of New York have 2-3 bathrooms, 3-5 bedrooms, are between 1000 and 4000 square footage, and are on a lot of less than 1 acre. From the data between 2019 and 2021, the average cost of housing was significantly more expensive in the Southeastern corner of New York, around the New York City and Long Island areas.

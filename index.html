Does Population Have an Impact on Housing Prices?

Sparked by curiosity about the housing boom that seemed to occur during the covid-19 pandemic, specifically in popular states such as California, Florida, New York, Texas, and then our home state of Tennessee, we took a look at the data between January 2019 and December 2021 to find the relationship between housing prices and population. We will input this data into a Linear Regression Machine Learning Model to predict the housing prices for these states in future years.

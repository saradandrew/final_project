Does Population Have an Impact on Housing Prices?

Sparked by curiosity about the housing boom that seemed to occur during the covid-19 pandemic, we took a look at the data between January 2019 and December 2021 to find the relationship between housing prices and population. We will input this data into a Linear Regression Machine Learning Model to predict future housing prices for New York in both low and high population cities.
